FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the CA program and who is a Campus Ambassador?
The Campus Ambassador will be the representative of Wissenaire, IIT Bhubaneswar in their campus and will help in publicising events and workshops conducted by Wissenaire.
What is the criteria to become a CA?
If you are a student pursuing either B.tech or B.E and if you have a valid college ID you can apply for this program.
How much time or effort from my part would be required?
Not more than 1 hour per week would be required from a CA.
Can there be multiple College Ambassadors from a single college?
Yes, depending on the size of the college, Wissenaire can appoint multiple CAs.
How will my progress be monitored?
You will upload proofs of your work on the CA portal; we will verify them and reward you with points that measure your progress.
